## Upwork/Fiverr Go-to-Market Plan
1. **Profile tagline:** “Full-stack AI/MCP engineer – built PayFlix (staging.payflix.fun) & SeekerXAI (seekerxai.com) for X402 hackathon (Visa/Coinbase/Solana).”
2. **Overview:** highlight demos, domain experience (NoOnes/Paxful), 7-day prototype promise.
3. **Visual proof:** Screenshots, Loom videos, GitHub references.
4. **Starter offer:** e.g., “Prototype your MCP/AI API with dashboards in 7 days.”
5. **Kickstart reviews:** take 1–2 smaller gigs at $100–$120/hr to capture 5-star ratings, then raise rates.
6. **Custom proposals:** reference PayFlix/SeekerXAI architecture, specify how you’d solve the client’s problem.
7. **Risk mitigation:** offer Phase 1 (discovery + architecture doc) as a fixed-fee deliverable.
8. **Testimonials:** ask Ray/NoOnes or past merchants for endorsements you can cite.

Once you have initial reviews, your profile becomes a standout because you’re offering proven systems, not just abstract skills.
